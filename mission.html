Mission

“Alone we can do so little, together we can do so much.” -Helen Keller

Our mission statement reflects our vision to connect the international community of machine learning programmers. To fulfill this vision, we have created a collaborative forum to integrate the creativity and innovation of our users. Users can post their models in the forum and receive feedback from friendly moderators and other users. All code is published in our forum under an open-source license to ensure accessibility for all.
