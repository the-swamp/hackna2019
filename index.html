About Us

We are a team of 5 high school juniors from North Allegheny Senior High School. Our mobile app, Neural Network Shell, allows you to test Tensorflow models in a secure environment by simply uploading the model file and any necessary data. This greatly expedites the development of machine learning projects by eliminating the need to use convoluted, expensive cloud services controlled by monopolistic corporations.
