    • Our Project
    Posted by Dev Team
    We are a team of 5 high school juniors from North Allegheny Senior High School. Our mobile app, Neural Network Shell, allows you to test Tensorflow models in a secure environment by simply uploading the model file and any necessary data. This greatly expedites the development of machine learning projects by eliminating the need to use convoluted, expensive cloud services controlled by monopolistic corporations. We have also created a collaborative forum to integrate the creativity and innovation of our users. Our mission statement, “Alone we can do so little, together we can do so much,” reflects our vision to connect the international community of machine learning programmers. Users can post their models in the forum and receive feedback from friendly moderators and other users. All code is published in our forum under an open-source license to ensure accessibility for all. In the future, we would like to improve the reliability, ease of use, and scope of our app to make it more competitive with current solutions. We would also like to increase the functionality of our web forum by implementing a clustering algorithm that suggests similar models to a user after making a post, providing him/her with new insight into their project.
